Crystal Collector!

How To Play


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each will be given a random value at the start of the game. By clicking the crystals, you will add their value to your score.

The values of the crystals will be hidden until you click them, and each new game will give each crystal a new random value.

Match your total score to the given number and win! Go over the given number and lose.
